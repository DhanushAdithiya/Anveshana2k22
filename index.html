What is Anveshana?

Talent, a gift that you are born with. A piece of you that no one could ever steal or forger. It is the shadow you carry around with you; no matter where you go, no matter how you grow. It is as much your passion as it is your responsibility. It is a road that's taken less often. So would you dare to...? We bring to you a platform, agolden chance for you to showcase your raw skills. An opportunity like no other to prove yourselves among the masses. Anveshana is a wonderful platform, an Intra-collegiate fest spread over a week, organised by UKTI - The Cultural Forum, and Nucleus - The Science Forum of School of Sciences, Jain (deemed-to-be University). A three day spectrum followed by a two day main event. Come forward, we call thee!

THEME

The age old Japanese tradition of art, passed down through aeons. The indestructible sapling, slashed through blades, coloured by dyes and folded with love. The representation of forever condensed to a paper. Borrowed from the land of the rising sun, Famed in the Silk Streets of Han and Worshipped in the Fortress of Gyeongju. Ori meaning folding, Kami meaning paper. It represents the Soul. The very core of you that is mobile. The part of you that sustains. The dust of you that never settles. It represents love, perseverance, success, loss, wisdom, war, adventure and hope. It represents the Sakura blossoms.

開花期が短いため、桜は生命そのもののメタファーです
Translating to 'How Sakura blossoms represent life, ever fleeting but beautiful. Ever changing but fragile.'
