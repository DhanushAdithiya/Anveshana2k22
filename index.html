What is Anveshana

Talent, a gift that you are born with. A piece of you that no one could ever steal or forger. It is the shadow you carry around with you; no matter where you go, no matter how you grow. It is as much your passion as it is your responsibility. It is a road that's taken less often. So would you dare to...?

We bring to you a platform, agolden chance for you to showcase your raw skills. An opportunity like no other to prove yourselves among the masses. Anveshana is a wonderful platform, an Intra-collegiate fest spread over a week, organised by UKTI - The Cultural Forum, and Nucleus - The Science Forum of School of Sciences, Jain (deemed-to-be University). A three day spectrum followed by a two day main event. Come forward, we call thee!
